About me

Front-end developer from Shrewsbury, England

I had never been one for the world of computers, the typical person who gets scared when their computer throws an error, or if anybody mentioned anything more complicated than opening the browser. But then I thought that 28 years old is a bit too young to feel like a dinosaur about anything!

Then I found SheCodes, an easy, extremely enjoyable way to learn something I had always thought was out of my reach. It's been one of the best experiences ever so far. During the day, I make a living teaching English as a foreign language, and writing legal arguments in defense of intellectual property. But by night I have a clandestine life as an aspiring Front-end developer!
